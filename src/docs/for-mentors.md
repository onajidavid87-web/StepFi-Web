# For Mentors

Mentors (also called vouchers) are senior developers who vouch for learners. Your endorsement boosts a learner's reputation score, unlocking lower interest rates and higher credit limits.

## How Vouching Works

1. A learner requests a vouch from you
2. You review their profile and loan application
3. If you believe in them, you sign a vouch transaction
4. Their reputation score increases immediately
5. They unlock better loan terms

## Vouch Tiers

| Tier | Score Range | Reputation Boost | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bronze | 60-74 | +6 points | 3 months |
| Silver | 75-89 | +12 points | 6 months |
| Gold | 90-100 | +18 points | 12 months |

## The Vouch Transaction

When you vouch for a learner, you sign a Stellar transaction:

```typescript

const txXdr = 'AAAAAgAAAABz...' // Generated by StepFi API

const signedXdr = 'signedTxXdr' in result
```

## Reputation Impact

Your own reputation is affected by the learners you vouch for:

- **Learner repays on time** → Your reputation is unaffected or improves
- **Learner defaults** → Your reputation decreases proportionally
- **Active vouch count** → Displayed on your mentor profile

## Managing Vouches

From the `/vouch` page you can:

- **View pending requests** — See learners who have requested a vouch
- **Review profiles** — Check learner history, scores, and loan purposes
- **Submit vouch** — Sign and submit a vouch transaction
- **Revoke vouch** — Remove your endorsement (reduces learner's boost)

```typescript
interface VouchRequest {
  learnerAddress: string
  learnerWallet: string
  score: number
  tier: 'Starter' | 'Bronze' | 'Silver' | 'Gold'
  loanAmount: number
  purpose: string
  skills: string[]
}
```

## Best Practices

- Only vouch for learners you genuinely believe will repay
- Review their skill set and loan purpose
- Check their repayment history if available
- Set expectations with learners about the responsibility
